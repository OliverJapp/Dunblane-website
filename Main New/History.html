As most people will know, Dunblane has its very own Olympic gold medal winner - tennis player, Andy Murray. And we also have a gold post box. Andy won gold in the mens singles tennis in London in 2012 and in Rio in 2016.


The golden postbox is an official location on trip advisor with over 400 reviews and is considered the #2 thing to visit in dunblane


On 13th of march a local man shot dead 16 Kids and their teacher with a firearm, The dunblane centre was founded by donations and now host community activities.


The Dunblane centre offers activities aimed at younger audiences but also allows local groups to rent out areas of the centre

Dunblane Cathedral is remarkable in having retained more of its late-medieval choir stalls than any other Scottish church building (except King's College Chapel, Aberdeen), and also is noted for its organ.
