Renovate Animos translates to "Renew your courage"


Dunblane or in Scottish Gaelic: Dùn Bhlàthain is a small town in the stirling council area in central Scotland, and inside the county of Perthshire.

It is a commuter town, with a small highstreet where local buisnesses thrive, there is a lovely country walk round the entirety of Dunblane as it's situated just outside of the city of Stirling.


Dunblane is built on the banks of the Allan Water. Dunblane Cathedral is its most prominent landmark.

Dunblane had a population of 8,114 in the 2001 census, which grew to 8,811 in the 2011 census. There are three primary schools, and one High school. Dunblane High School has around 1000 pupils, it is currently the best public school in all of Scotland.
